YourCoach exports daily, weekly, and monthly analytical spreadsheets to the
Company S3 or SFTP directory to monitor participation and billing cycles.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="program_pair — Daily">
    Displays current program lifecycle statuses and coach pairings.
  </Accordion>

<Accordion title="sessions — Daily">
  Complete data log of scheduled, missed, and attended live video sessions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="chat_transcript_data — Daily">
  Message-level text transcript logs separated by user/coach roles.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="course_restart_dates — Daily">
  Log of all programs that were renewed, extended, or restarted on this day.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="coaching_engagement — Weekly">
  Summarizes sessions, missed meetings, and message exchange volumes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="coaching_program_summary — Weekly">
  High-level overview: total enrolled users, total sessions, and program
  duration.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="client_billing_reconciliation — Monthly">
  Comprehensive billing details (new enrolments, re-enrolments, active billing
  cycles).
</Accordion>

  <Accordion title="sessions_and_program_cycles — Monthly">
    Structural logging of session allocations mapped to specific program cycles.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
